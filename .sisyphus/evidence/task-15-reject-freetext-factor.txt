## Non-Ref Factor Rejection — relation-intent-resolver.ts

File: src/memory/cognition/relation-intent-resolver.ts

### New Validation in resolveConflictFactors()
Before attempting nodeRef resolution, each ConflictFactor is validated:

1. Empty/missing `kind`:
   - Condition: !factor.kind || factor.kind.trim().length === 0
   - Result: rejected with reason "missing or empty kind"
   - Logged: [settlement_conflict_factor_rejected] reason=missing_kind

2. Empty/missing `ref`:
   - Condition: !factor.ref || factor.ref.trim().length === 0
   - Result: rejected with reason "missing or empty ref (freetext factor rejected)"
   - Logged: [settlement_conflict_factor_rejected] reason=missing_ref

3. Non-resolvable ref (freetext strings):
   - Condition: ref doesn't match any graph node ref pattern, cognition key, or local ref
   - Result: rejected with reason "unresolvable ref: {ref}"
   - No conflicts_with edge created
