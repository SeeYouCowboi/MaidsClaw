## Design Notes

CORS headers are applied at the server.ts level via `applyCors()` wrapping the
final response. This means SSE responses from `createSseStream()` automatically
get CORS headers without modifying sse.ts. The `new Response(res.body, ...)`
clone pattern preserves the ReadableStream body for SSE while merging headers.
