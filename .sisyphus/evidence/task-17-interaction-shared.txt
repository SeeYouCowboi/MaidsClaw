## Changes Made

### src/interaction/store.ts — db.raw removal
- Replaced `this.db.raw.inTransaction` with private `_inTransaction` boolean flag
- Replaced `this.db.raw.prepare("BEGIN IMMEDIATE").run()` → `this.db.exec("BEGIN IMMEDIATE")`
- Replaced `this.db.raw.prepare("COMMIT").run()` → `this.db.exec("COMMIT")`
- Replaced `this.db.raw.prepare("ROLLBACK").run()` → `this.db.exec("ROLLBACK")`
- Added `finally` blocks to ensure `_inTransaction` flag is always reset
- Replaced `this.db.raw.prepare(...)` in `upsertRecentCognitionSlot` → `this.db.run(sql, params)`
- Result: ZERO db.raw references remain in store.ts

### src/memory/shared-blocks/shared-block-attach-service.ts — DbLike removal
