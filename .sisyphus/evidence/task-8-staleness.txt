- Uses settlementUnitOfWork.run() for read-only getVersionGap() calls
- Three-tier logic:

- Config values sourced from this.talkerThinkerConfig (constructor param, NOT file)
- Settlement payload includes cognitionVersionGap and usedStaleState
- ownerAgentId resolved once (reused for gap check and settlement)
